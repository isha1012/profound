The conceptual framework deals with the relationship between the dependent and the independent variables of study. The independent variables are the explanatory variables used in explaining the variations in the dependent variable as the value of the dependent variable depends solely on the independent variables. The independent variables will include legal policies, supplier partnership and lean inventory of the cement industry while the dependent variable is the performance of procurement function of cement manufacturing companies in Dangote Cement manufacturing company. The relationship that exists between the dependent variable and the sets of independent variables under study are of great importance as it helps in achieving the main aim of this study which is the effect of inventory management on the performance of the procurement function of Dangote cement manufacturing company
